== Description ==

Looking to create a mobile app for your WooCommerce store or WordPress blog without any coding? The **Build App Online** plugin allows you to build a fully functional mobile app in just 5 minutes, with no technical expertise required. Whether you're managing an e-commerce business, a grocery store, or a fashion website, this plugin is designed to convert your WordPress website into an engaging mobile experience.

With **Build App Online**, you can easily create mobile apps for both Android and iOS, customize app themes, and integrate advanced features like Google login, OTP authentication, and push notifications. The plugin offers seamless integration with WooCommerce, allowing you to sync products, orders, and payments with your mobile app.

**Steps to Build Your Mobile App:**

1. Install this plugin.
2. Visit [https:/apps.taqnix.com/build](https:/apps.taqnix.com/build).
3. Enter your purchase code:  
   * **BUILDAPPONLINE** (for WooCommerce)  
   * **BUILDBLOGAPP** (for WordPress Blog)  
   * **BUILDWEBAPP** (for Web App)  
4. Enter your site URL.
5. Click "Continue."
6. Enter your app name.
7. Provide your WooCommerce consumer key and secret.
8. Upload your app logo, icon, and splash screen.
9. (Optional) Enter Google Maps API key.
10. Upload GoogleService.json (for Firebase).
11. Select APK for testing or AppBundle for Play Store submission.
12. Click "Save" and "Build."
13. Download your app and upload it to the Play Store.

**Steps to Customize Your Mobile App:**

1. Download "Design My App" from the Play Store [here](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.buildapp.appdesign&hl=en_IN).
2. Or, download it from the Apple Store [here](https://apps.apple.com/in/app/design-my-app/id1569479709).
3. Go to "Settings" in the app.
4. Add your website.
5. Log in with your site’s admin/editor credentials.
6. Customize the app theme, colors, app bar, and buttons etc.
7. Save the changes.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress.
3. Follow the steps in the description to create your mobile app.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I create a mobile app for free? =  
Yes, you can build apps online up to 5 times for a single site for free.

= How can I create a mobile app? =  
Follow our YouTube guides to build your app:  
* [Build App Online](https://youtu.be/B16izmXiKhM)

= Google login and OTP login not working =  
You must integrate Firebase into your app. Google login and OTP login do not work in test APKs. These features will function properly when you upload the app bundle to the Play Store and integrate Firebase. Watch our [Firebase integration video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Jquy-dOyz34).

= Play Store Error: Your app requests android.permission.CAMERA. Apps using this permission require a privacy policy =  
Ensure you add a privacy policy on the Play Store. In the Play Console, navigate to Policy > App content > Privacy Policy and provide the URL of your privacy policy.

= Wallet not working =  
Install the TeraWallet plugin (version 1.3.10) for wallet functionality. Download it [here](https://downloads.wordpress.org/plugin/woo-wallet.1.3.10.zip).

= How does refer and earn work? =  
Refer and earn works with Dynamic Links and the TeraWallet plugin.  
Follow our [Firebase dynamic link setup](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Jquy-dOyz34) and install TeraWallet (version 1.3.10).
